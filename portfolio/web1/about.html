About Prestige Electric

Welcome to Prestige electric, one of the largest highly specialised services consulting company dealing with power generation and high voltage. We have been established in 2010, we have celebrated 13 years of success in providing unique value proposition around the world and giving access to optimized power

Prestige Electric is a 60 % black owned, 10 % black women owned and 30 percent white owned.

Vision

Our vision is to lead our customers towards improved and efficient electrical network systems, enable and assisted by the latest inventions of technology and highly competent engineers.

Mission

Our in-depth understanding of electricity allows us to identify new innovative methods which assist in implementing new designs which will optimize power.

Through continued research and education, we ensure that our engineers remain at the forefront of workplace technology. They continue giving impeccable designs and solving all hazardous situation.
